# Prompt: Build a Premium Mobile-First Graduation Thank You Website

Create a **premium, mobile-first responsive website** dedicated to expressing gratitude to everyone who attended and celebrated my graduation ceremony. The website should provide a memorable, elegant, and emotional experience while maintaining the quality and aesthetics of a high-end commercial product.

The design should feel sophisticated, modern, and interactive, featuring smooth animations, premium UI/UX, and professional automation throughout the entire experience.

---

# Website Theme

**"Thank You for Celebrating My Graduation"**

A heartfelt digital experience that expresses appreciation to family, friends, professors, classmates, and everyone who made my graduation journey special.

The website should tell a beautiful story—from the beginning of my academic journey to graduation day—and conclude with a sincere message of gratitude.

---

# Design Style

* Mobile-First Design
* Fully Responsive (Phone, Tablet, Desktop)
* Modern Minimal Luxury
* Glassmorphism + Soft Gradient
* Elegant White and Gold Theme with Navy Blue Accents
* Smooth Micro-interactions
* Premium Typography (Inter, Poppins, or SF Pro Display)
* Cinematic scrolling experience
* Plenty of whitespace
* Professional layout similar to Apple's event pages
